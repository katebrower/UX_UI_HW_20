Corks Wine Tasting App

Group project: Kate Brower, Hyebin Han, Sebastian Martinez

Corks curates wine suggestions and education for an in-home or virtual tasting experience to bring people together in a shared, social environment.

Problem Statement

Busy working mom who enjoys bringing people together at home and drinking wine but doesn’t stray from the wine she knows (her “go-to’s”) due to limited knowledge of wine and food pairing, needs a socially distant outlet help to broaden her wine and pairing know-how because wine education is more fun and fulfilling in a social setting.
